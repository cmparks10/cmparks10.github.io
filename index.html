GRAVERIPPER

GRAVERIPPER is a thrash metal band with black metal influence from Indianapolis, Indiana. Started in 2017 by frontman Corey Parks as a studio project, the band quickly assembled a live lineup and began playing shows. The band released a three track EP in Spring of 2020 titled “Complete Blinding Darkness”. The band underwent a lineup change and began recording new material in January of 2021. The band was signed to WISE BLOOD RECORDS in February. The band released the “RADIATED REMAINS” EP in June of 2021. Mixed by Carl Byers (ex-Coffin Worm) at Clandestine Arts Recording and mastered by Joel Grind (Toxic Holocaust). The EP would garner praise from Decibel Magazine, Metal Injection, Toilet ov Hell, No Clean Singing (Twice), and would make several EP of the year lists, as well as becoming the best selling record on the label. This would result in the band supporting the release by performing in various locations in the midwest supporting acts such as RINGWORM, NUNSLAUGHTER, DEMISER, CLOAK, and BEWITCHER. In September of 2022 the band would release three songs for “FASTER THAN THE FUCKING DEVIL”, a four way split between GRAVERIPPER, WRAITH, BLACK KNIFE, and UNHOLY NIGHT. The band received praise for the three tracks in multiple outlets. This resulted in the band performing at Full Terror Assault Festival, a mini tour through Milwaukee, Chicago, and Ft. Wayne, and flying to Los Angeles, California to perform at Charged Noise Fest, a three day festival of black/thrash metal alongside WRAITH, BEWITCHER, and BAT. The band has currently been working on their debut full length LP, which is slated to begin tracking in early 2023. We are active on Facebook at facebook.com/graveripperofficial, Instagram @grave.ripper.metal, and Bandcamp at graveripper.bandcamp.com.
